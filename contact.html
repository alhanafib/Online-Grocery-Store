Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about FreshMart

How fast is your delivery?

We offer delivery within 30 minutes in most urban areas. For suburban areas, delivery typically takes 45-60 minutes. You can track your order in real-time through our app or website.

What is your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with any product, you can return it within 24 hours for a full refund. Simply contact our customer service team, and we'll arrange a pickup and refund.

Do you offer organic products?

Yes! We have a wide selection of organic fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and pantry staples. Look for the "Organic" badge on our products when shopping online.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also log into your account on our website or app to see real-time updates on your delivery.
